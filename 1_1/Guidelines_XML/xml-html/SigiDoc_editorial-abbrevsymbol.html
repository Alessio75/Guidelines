Expansion of symbol

Leiden symbols

  • Editorial interpretation: (Ὁ ἅγιος)
  • Diplomatic edition: none

Explanation

Using Athena Ruby for the diplomatic edition means that the tagging for the editorial interpretation could be very limited: 

                    <expan>
                        <ex>Ὁ ἅγιος</ex>
                    </expan>

Note that abbr is not used in this case.

If Athena Ruby was not used, a tagging a bit more elaborated would be required, with am and g in the legend, and charDecl: 

                    <expan>
                        <abbr>
                            <am>
                                <g ref="#hoHagios"></g>
                            </am>
                        </abbr>
                        <ex>Ὁ ἅγιος</ex>
                    </expan>

In this case the abbr element will be used, with the addition of am (abbreviation mark), to indicate that the symbol, while part of the abbreviation, is not part of the resolved word (i.e. the symbol in g will not be visualised).
