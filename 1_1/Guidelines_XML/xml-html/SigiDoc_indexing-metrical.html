Metrical legends

Elements

  • <lg/>

Explanation

Important: lg should be specified by a type attribute, whose value(s) listed here are the only being currently handled by SigiDoc/EFES autority lists and they should be used as they appear here: 

                    <lg type="metrical" ana=""><l/></lg>

Notice that here the attribute pointing to the authority list is ana (and not ref).

If the metrical legend is spanned over the two faces of the seal, lg should be duplicated on the 2 faces (because the latter are encoded in two separate divs) for a proper tagging, but in the second the ref to the authority list shouldnâ€™t be added, otherwise the index would display 2 entries for the same metrical legend.

See also "Verse lines".
