Illegible lines: quantity known

Leiden symbols

  • Editorial interpretation: - - - - - - - - - -
  • Diplomatic edition: none

Explanation

                    <gap reason="illegible" quantity="1" unit="line"/>

Vestiges of lines visible but illegible.

Note: the quantity's value does not affect the visualisation; for this reason, this tag should be repeated for each illegible line, after lb, with a fixed quantity’s value of “1”.
