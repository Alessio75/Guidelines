Iconography

Elements

  • <figDesc/>

Explanation

                    <figDesc n="whole" ana="iconography.xml#...">Bust of St George</figDesc>

Designed for general subjets as in figDesc general, with n="whole" (for indexing only, not displayed after transformation).

Notice that here the attribute pointing to the authority list is ana (and not ref) and that there is not type attribute.
